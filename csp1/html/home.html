Art Capital of the Philippines

Angono is known as the country’s art capital, and rightly so. It is the birthplace of National Artists Carlos “Botong” Francisco and Lucio San Pedro. And, not only does it have works of renowned artists at different art galleries and museums which you can easily spend all day exploring, it also has art right on its streets, starting from the "Malakas and Maganda" statues outside the town hall to the sculpted murals on walls and gates of residents’ houses.
--Rhea Claire Madarang
